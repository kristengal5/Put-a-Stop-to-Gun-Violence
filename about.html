What is gun violence?

Gun violence threatens the right to life and causes tragedies around the world.
What are mass shootings?

Mass shootings are shootings where 4+ victims are killed.

156 mass shootings and 848 victims from 2009-2016.

315 gunfire incidents on US school grounds From 2013-2018.

more mass shootings in the past 5 years than any other half-decade since 1966.

Tulsa

easy access to firearms

lack of regulation

poor implementation of existing laws
